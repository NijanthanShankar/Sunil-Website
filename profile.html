Dr. Sunil Eshwar

MBBS, MD (JIPMER), DNB, PGDHS, DIP. Gyn Laparoscopy (Germany)

Dr. Sunil Eshwar, M.B.B.S. MD, DNB, is a renowned Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon, Obstetrician and Gynaecological Surgeon with more than a decade of experience in the field. Dr. Sunil Eshwar, graduated of Bangalore Medical College in the year 2000.

He passed out his post-graduation in Obstetrics and Gynaecology from the prestigious Jawahar Institute of Medical Education and Research,(JIPMER) Puducherry.

He is currently Medical director of Aikya Fertility And Research Center, which is a specialty center for fertility treatments and a training center for budding fertility specialist and embryologist. He is also the lead consultant gynecologist and laparoscopic surgeon at Aster RV Hospitals. His area of expertise includes the management of high risk pregnancies, infertility and laparoscopic surgeries. In infertility he has keen interest and very good success rates in treating failed IVF couples.

He is vastly experienced in the field of fertility treatment with more than 1500 IVF cycles. He is also an experienced Laparoscopic Surgeon with penchant interest in difficult cases specifically endometriosis. He is also very experienced surgeon in fertility preserving Surgeries, Hysteroscopic Surgeries. He has performed more than 8000, major Laparoscopic Surgeries.
