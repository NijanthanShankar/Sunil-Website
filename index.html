Dr. Sunil Eshwar

MBBS, MD (JIPMER), DNB, PGDHS, DIP. Gyn Laparoscopy (Germany)

Top Obstetrics and Gynecology Specialist in Bangalore

Dr. Sunil Eshwar is a renowned Advanced Laparoscopic Surgeon, Obstetrician and Gynecological Surgeon with over a decade of experience in the field. He graduated from Bangalore Medical College in 2000 and completed his post-graduation in Obstetrics and Gynaecology at the prestigious Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER), Puducherry.

Dr. Sunil Eshwar is recognized as one of the top gynecologists and obstetrician in Bangalore, known for his expertise in complex obstetrics and gynecological surgeries. His patient-centric approach and dedication to advanced care make him a trusted name in women's health.
